Life Case 2: Negative Group Dynamics


Reflection and discussion answers


  • Clearly, the boys are trying to get attention. So, in this case, does ignoring them be helpful?

    In most of the cases, involving a group seeking for attention, ignoring them would be the first option to do. If it does not work, the next solution would be to talk to the boys.
  • What is the best way to handle the situation?

    Sometimes, ignoring the students can mislead them and takes the teacher for granted. Tlaking to the boys and telling them that their behaviour is not advisable can make a change.
  • For such students, most of the time, the reason could be the lack of attention at home. Do you consider that?

    Advising the boys not to disturb the class at first is a better way. If they not listen, it is a better way to find the reason behind their acting out which could the situations at home.
